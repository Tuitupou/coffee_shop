Our Story

Founded in 2011 by the Tuitupou family in Tacoma, Washington, our coffee shop began as a simple dream to share our love for quality coffee with our community. What started as a small family operation has grown into a beloved local institution, serving not just coffee, but creating connections and memories.

Our journey from Tacoma to Seattle and soon to Olympia represents more than just business expansion—it's about bringing our family's warmth and commitment to quality to more communities across Washington state.

Our Values

  • Family First: Every decision we make reflects our family values and commitment to treating customers like family.
  • Sustainability: We partner with farms that practice sustainable agriculture and fair labor practices.
  • Quality: From bean selection to brewing, we never compromise on the quality of our coffee.
  • Community: We're committed to being active, supportive members of every community we serve.

Our Promise

When you visit Tuitupou Coffee, you're not just getting a cup of coffee—you're becoming part of our family story. We promise to serve you with the same care and attention we'd give to our own family members.
